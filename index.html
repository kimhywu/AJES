About Antonio

I am a Lecturer at the Department of Chemical Engineering, University of Bath. My research interests focus on two main areas: catalyst design and reactor engineering. In particular, the development of new hybrid photo-electrocatalysts and microreactors which can be applied in environmental processes as artificial photosynthesis.
